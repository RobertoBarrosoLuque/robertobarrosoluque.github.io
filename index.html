    • About

    Born in Mexico City currently living in Chicago. I graduated from Boston University in 2017 with degrees in Neuroscience and Economics. Post bacc I worked as a research technician at the amazing Harvey Lab , where I investigated neo-cortical circuits involved in working memory and spatial navigation in mice.

    After two years in neurobiology I decided to shift gears and went into graduate school in Computer Science and Public Policy. I spent a summer as an intern with the great folks of Paylocity's Data Science Department and currently work as a Data Scientist at Omelasdefend democracy from digital propaganda.
