A tad about me

Hello friend of the virtual world. I am Roberto!
Im currently a graduate student at UChicago and a Machine Learning Engineer at Omelas.

Born in Mexico City currently living in Chicago. I graduated from Boston University in 2017. Post bacc I worked as a research technician at the amazing Harvey Lab, where I investigated neo-cortical circuits involved in working memory and spatial navigation in mice.

After two years in neurobiology I decided to shift gears and went into graduate school in Computer Science and Public Policy. This past summer I interned with the great folks of Paylocity's Data Science Department.
